About Me

Welcome to my website!

I just graduated from Mount Holyoke College with BA in Computer Science and Mathematics. Currently, I am looking for a SWE position and my earliest starting date is mid-October 2019. My primary programming languages are Java and Python and I am also familiar with R and C. Please check out my selected projects here or on GitHub.

This summer I just attended Google Applied Machine Learning Intensive held at the Scripps cohort. I gained a deeper understanding of ML and its application area, and I finished a final project about Bitcoin price prediction with three of my classmates. I really appreciate this opportunity to learn more ML models, get to know more friends, and enjoy the California sunshine! I highly recommend this program.

I am one of the found members of the Chinese Music Ensemble at Mount Holyoke College. As a member of Glee Club and Vocal Jazz, I really love singing. Cantonese song is my love. Check out my song list on Spotify.
